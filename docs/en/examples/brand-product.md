# Brand product pack

```text
acme-widget/
├── KNOWLEDGE.md
├── sources/
│   ├── product-one-pager.md
│   ├── pricing.md
│   └── compliance.md
└── compiled/
    ├── facts.md
    ├── voice.md
    ├── playbook.md
    └── boundaries.md
```

## Use cases

- product pages
- sales emails
- social posts
- partner briefs
- support replies

## Boundary examples

- Do not make medical, financial, or regulated claims without source anchors.
- Do not invent pricing or availability.
- Prefer approved positioning from `compiled/voice.md`.
